In Compliance With 45 CFR §§164.312(a), 164.312(d)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

2  TO SAFEGUARD ACCESS TO EPHI THROUGH SECURE AUTHENTICATION AND PASSWORD STANDARDS.

3  POLICY STATEMENTS

Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

Default vendor or system passwords must be changed immediately.

4  MULTI-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (MFA) IS REQUIRED FOR REMOTE OR PRIVILEGED ACCESS.

Shared or generic accounts are prohibited.

5  RESPONSIBILITIES

6  IT DEPARTMENT: ENFORCE PASSWORD POLICIES AND MONITOR AUTHENTICATION SYSTEMS.

7  WORKFORCE MEMBERS: PROTECT PASSWORDS AND REPORT SUSPECTED CREDENTIAL COMPROMISE IMMEDIATELY.

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
