In Compliance with §164.502(e), §164.308(b)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

2  TO ENSURE VENDORS AND BUSINESS ASSOCIATES WITH PHI ACCESS COMPLY WITH HIPAA REQUIREMENTS.

3  SCOPE
 APPLIES TO ALL THIRD-PARTY VENDORS, CONTRACTORS, AND SERVICE PROVIDERS HANDLING PHI.

4  POLICY

5  A SIGNED BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT (BAA) IS REQUIRED BEFORE PHI ACCESS.

6  VENDORS MUST UNDERGO SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE DUE DILIGENCE BEFORE ENGAGEMENT.

7  VENDOR COMPLIANCE WILL BE REVIEWED ANNUALLY.

Upon contract termination, vendors must return or securely destroy PHI.

8  RESPONSIBILITIES

9  COMPLIANCE OFFICER: ENSURE BAAS ARE EXECUTED AND REVIEWS OF THEM ARE COMPLETED ANNUALLY.

10  PROCUREMENT / MANAGEMENT: CONFIRM VENDOR OVERSIGHT PROCESSES ARE FOLLOWED.

11  REVIEW AND REVISION

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
