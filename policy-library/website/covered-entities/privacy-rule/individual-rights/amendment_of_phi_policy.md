In compliance with 45 CFR §164.526

1  POLICY

Individuals may request amendments to their PHI in writing.

The Privacy Officer will review requests and respond within 60 days. A one-time 30-day extension is allowed if necessary.

If the amendment is accepted, the organization will update the designated record set and notify relevant third parties.

If the amendment is denied, the individual will receive written notice explaining the reasons and may submit a written statement of disagreement, which must be appended to the record.
