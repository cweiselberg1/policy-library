# Physical Safeguards Audit - Quick Start Guide

## Access the Tool

**URL:** [http://localhost:3000/audit/physical](http://localhost:3000/audit/physical) (dev)

**From Homepage:**
1. Scroll to "Everything You Need" section
2. Click the "Physical Safeguards Audit" card
3. Click "Start Audit" button

## Using the Audit Tool

### Step 1: Start Assessment
- Tool loads with 4 main sections
- Left sidebar shows your progress
- Auto-save keeps your work safe

### Step 2: Answer Questions
For each question:
- **Read** the question text and guidance
- **Select** Yes (compliant) / No (gap) / N/A (not applicable)
- **Add notes** if needed (optional)
- Progress saves automatically

### Step 3: Navigate Sections
- **Next Section** button to advance
- **Previous** button to go back
- **Click section names** in sidebar to jump around
- Watch progress bars fill up!

### Step 4: Complete Audit
- When 100% complete, **"Complete Audit"** button appears
- Click to view your results

### Step 5: Review Results
- **Overall Score** shows compliance percentage
- **Section Scores** show where to focus
- **Recommendations** list gaps to address (prioritized)
- **Detailed Responses** shows all your answers

### Step 6: Export Report
- Click **"Export Report"** button
- Markdown file downloads automatically
- Share with your team or keep for records

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I save my progress and return later?**
A: Yes! Progress auto-saves. Close and return anytime.

**Q: What if I'm not sure about an answer?**
A: Use N/A and add a note. You can update later.

**Q: Can I change my answers?**
A: Yes! Navigate back to any question and update it.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Your responses only exist in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to servers.

**Q: Can I print the report?**
A: Export to Markdown, then convert to PDF or print from your editor.

## Reset Your Audit

To start fresh:
1. Click **"Reset Progress"** button
2. Confirm the warning
3. All responses cleared
