In Compliance with 45 CFR §164.310(b)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

2  TO GOVERN THE PROPER FUNCTIONS AND PHYSICAL PLACEMENT OF WORKSTATIONS THAT ACCESS PHI.

3  POLICY STATEMENTS

4  WORKSTATIONS MUST ONLY BE USED FOR AUTHORIZED BUSINESS PURPOSES.

Workstations must be positioned to prevent unauthorized viewing of PHI.

Workforce members must log off or lock screens when workstations are left unattended.

PHI must not be downloaded or stored locally unless explicitly approved.

5  RESPONSIBILITIES

6  IT DEPARTMENT: CONFIGURE WORKSTATIONS WITH ACCESS CONTROLS AND AUTOMATIC TIMEOUTS.

7  WORKFORCE MEMBERS: FOLLOW WORKSTATION USE REQUIREMENTS AND REPORT VIOLATIONS.

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
