In compliance with 45 CFR §164.522(a)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY:
TO OUTLINE HOW THE ORGANIZATION HANDLES PATIENT REQUESTS TO RESTRICT THE USE OR DISCLOSURE OF PHI.

2  POLICY STATEMENTS

Individuals may request restrictions on the use or disclosure of PHI for treatment, payment, or healthcare operations.

3  ALL REQUESTS MUST BE IN WRITING AND DIRECTED TO THE COMPLIANCE OFFICER.

The organization is not required to agree to most requests, but must comply when the individual has paid in full out-of-pocket and requests that the information not be shared with a health plan.

Approved restrictions must be documented and communicated to affected workforce members.

4  RESPONSIBILITIES

5  PRIVACY OFFICER: EVALUATE REQUESTS, DOCUMENT DECISIONS, AND ENSURE COMPLIANCE WITH REQUIRED RESTRICTIONS.

6  WORKFORCE MEMBERS: ADHERE STRICTLY TO APPROVED RESTRICTIONS.

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
