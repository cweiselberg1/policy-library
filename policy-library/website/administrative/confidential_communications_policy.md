In compliance with 45 CFR §164.522(b)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

2  TO PROTECT INDIVIDUAL PRIVACY BY HONORING REASONABLE REQUESTS FOR ALTERNATIVE MEANS OR LOCATIONS OF COMMUNICATION.

3  POLICY STATEMENTS

Individuals may request that PHI be communicated by alternative means (e.g., via mail instead of phone) or at alternative locations (e.g., P.O. box instead of home address).

Requests must be in writing and will be accommodated if reasonable.

The organization will not require individuals to explain the reason for the request.

4  RESPONSIBILITIES

5  PRIVACY OFFICER: APPROVE, DOCUMENT, AND CONFIGURE SYSTEMS FOR CONFIDENTIAL COMMUNICATIONS.

6  WORKFORCE MEMBERS: FOLLOW DOCUMENTED COMMUNICATION PREFERENCES.

7  REVIEW AND REVISION

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
