In Compliance with 45 CFR §164.308(a)(3)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

2  TO ENSURE ONLY AUTHORIZED WORKFORCE MEMBERS HAVE ACCESS TO PHI.

3  POLICY STATEMENTS

4  ACCESS TO PHI IS ROLE-BASED AND REQUIRES PRIOR APPROVAL.

Workforce members must undergo clearance and background verification as appropriate.

5  ACCESS WILL BE TERMINATED IMMEDIATELY UPON ROLE CHANGE OR SEPARATION FROM EMPLOYMENT.

6  SUPERVISORS MUST MONITOR AND ENFORCE THE APPROPRIATE ACCESS TO PHI.

7  RESPONSIBILITIES

8  HR DEPARTMENT: CONDUCT BACKGROUND CHECKS AND COORDINATE ONBOARDING/OFFBOARDING.

9  SUPERVISORS: AUTHORIZE ACCESS AND MONITOR WORKFORCE COMPLIANCE.

10  IT DEPARTMENT: PROVISION AND DEPROVISION ACCESS TO SYSTEMS.

11  REVIEW AND REVISION
