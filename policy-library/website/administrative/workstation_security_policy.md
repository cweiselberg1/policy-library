In Compliance with 45 CFR §164.310(c)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

2  TO IMPLEMENT PHYSICAL SAFEGUARDS THAT PREVENT UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS TO WORKSTATIONS ACCESSING PHI.

3  POLICY STATEMENTS

4  WORKSTATIONS MUST BE LOCATED IN SECURE AREAS WITH LIMITED ACCESS.

5  PHYSICAL LOCKS OR SECURITY CABLES MUST BE USED FOR PORTABLE DEVICES.

Screen savers and auto-lock features must be enabled after 10 minutes of inactivity.

6  RESPONSIBILITIES

7  IT DEPARTMENT: IMPLEMENT TECHNICAL SECURITY CONTROLS.

8  SUPERVISORS: ENSURE WORK AREAS HOUSING WORKSTATIONS ARE SECURE.

9  REVIEW AND REVISION

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
