Documentation & Records Retention

P O L I C Y

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

In Compliance with 45 CFR §§164.316(b), 164.530(j)

1  PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

To ensure all HIPAA-related policies, procedures, and actions are properly documented and retained for compliance.

2  POLICY STATEMENTS

All HIPAA documentation must be retained for at least six (6) years from the date of creation or last effective date.

3  DOCUMENTATION MUST BE ACCESSIBLE FOR AUDIT AND REGULATORY REVIEW.

Records must be stored securely, whether electronic or physical, to protect against unauthorized access or loss.

Responsibilities

4  COMPLIANCE OFFICER: MAINTAIN DOCUMENTATION, ENSURE TIMELY UPDATES, AND PROVIDE ACCESS DURING AUDITS.

5  ALL WORKFORCE MEMBERS: SUBMIT HIPAA COMPLIANCE RECORDS TO THE COMPLIANCE OFFICER FOR RETENTION.

6  REVIEW AND REVISION

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance with HIPAA regulations and organizational practices.
